Translocation breakpoints in K562 identified by HiNT

HiNT is a tool for identifying structural variants using Hi-C data. The list below contains the set of breakpoints called on the Hi-C map of K562 cells generated by Rao et al. 2014. Click on a breakpoint on the left to zoom in to its location in the Hi-C heatmap.
The top view zooms into the called breakpoints while the bottom view provides an overview of the entire genome. Visualizations produced using  HiGlass
